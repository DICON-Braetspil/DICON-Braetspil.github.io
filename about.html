About DICON Brætspil

DICON Brætspil is DIKU's (un)official student-driven board game association!

We are focused on bringing people together through tabletop gaming and social events.

We host game nights and other events for all SCIENCE students, whether they are a newcomer or veteran to board games.

Be sure to check out our board game list for a list of games we offer! We try to keep it updated.

DICON is currently run by its board as well as its amazing organizers. Please reach out to us at an event to hear more about joining us!
